You Plan a 5-day trip to Japan focusing on culture and food

I'd be happy to help you plan an amazing cultural and culinary journey through Japan! Here's a suggested 5-day itinerary:

Day 1-2: Tokyo

  • Visit Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa
  • Explore Tsukiji Outer Market for fresh sushi
  • Experience a traditional tea ceremony

Day 3: Kyoto

  • Tour Fushimi Inari shrine
  • Walk through Gion district
  • Try kaiseki multi-course dinner

Day 4-5: Osaka

  • Street food tour in Dotonbori
  • Visit Osaka Castle
  • Try takoyaki and okonomiyaki
You What's the best time of year to visit?

The best times to visit Japan are:

  • Spring (March-May): Cherry blossom season, mild weather
  • Fall (September-November): Beautiful autumn foliage, comfortable temperatures

Avoid Golden Week (late April/early May) and Obon (mid-August) when travel is crowded and expensive.
